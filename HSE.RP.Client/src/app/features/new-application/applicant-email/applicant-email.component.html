Your email address

We will email you a 6-digit code so you can verify your email address with us. We will use this email address if we need to contact you about your application, and to inform you of the outcome. If you lose access to this email address, you will have to begin your application again. Use your individual or personal email address to apply. You as an individual, not your organisation, will be registered as a building inspector. You will be able to add a second email address later. If you do not have an email address, contact us apply to register as a building inspector.

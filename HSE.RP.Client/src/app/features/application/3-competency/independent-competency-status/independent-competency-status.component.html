If you have not passed your competency assessment

If you have not passed assessment, you can still complete your registration. You should apply for the class you are being assessed for, but you will be registered as a class 1 building inspector.

You must pass your competency assessment by the deadline that applies to the country you will work in to continue working on class 2 and 3 projects. If you do not, you cannot continue to work on regulated building control activities and will be treated as a class 1 trainee RBI.

You can request to change your class of registration when you have completed a relevant competency assessment.

Class 2 specialist building inspector Inspection: which category of buildings are you competent to inspect?

Competent to inspect means you can inspect buildings to ensure they comply with submitted, assessed plans and associated information, and comply with building regulations.

Your category will be published on the register

To register as a class 4 technical manager you must be competent to do all BSR regulated activities (assessing plans and inspections) for all categories of class 2 or class 3 building inspectors.

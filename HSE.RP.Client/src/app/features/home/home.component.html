Register as a building inspector

Use this service to apply to register as a building inspector with the Building Safety Regulator (BSR). You must be registered with BSR to inspect buildings, sites and plans. This includes all buildings from domestic premises to complex high-rise multi-use structures. BSR will make public the register of building inspectors. It will contain details of the type of work you are competent to do. Your registration will be valid for 4 years.
